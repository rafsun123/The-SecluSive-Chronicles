    My Education

    I was born and raised in Chittagong and began my educational journey at The Dux School, where I studied from playgroup through class 10. I graduated in October 2021 after completing my GCE O Level Examinations with six subjects under the Cambridge International Examination board. I achieved 4A*s in Physics, Biology, Chemistry, and Mathematics, along with 2As in English Language and Additional Mathematics. In January 2022, I enrolled in Sunshine Grammar School (SGS) in Chittagong, Bangladeshâ€”the oldest school in the port city. SGS offers a prestigious merit-based scholarship for A Level students who achieve all A and A* grades in their O Levels. After earning 4A*s and 2As in my O Level exams, I was awarded a substantial scholarship covering around 100% of my tuition. SGS provided me with numerous resources and opportunities, allowing me to form lasting friendships, build strong connections with my teachers, and fully leverage the school's offerings. I took four subjects in the Cambridge International AS & A Levels: Biology, Chemistry, Physics, and Mathematics (including Mechanics, Pure Maths, and Statistics). With unwavering support from my teachers, friends, and family, I obtained all As in my AS Level exams held in October 2022, including 93% in Physics, 100% in Mechanics (92% in Mathematics overall), 93% in Chemistry, and 89% in Biology. After rigorous preparation through solving past papers and taking numerous mock tests, I achieved 3A*s and 1A in my A Level exams in October 2023, becoming one of the country's highest achievers. I will always be deeply grateful to my schools and teachers for guiding me on my academic journey. The Dux School and Sunshine Grammar School will forever stand as formidable institutions in my life.

    Activities

    My extracurricular activities reflect a deep commitment to community service, leadership, and creative expression. As a volunteer with the Bangladesh Red Crescent Society (BDRCS), I was actively involved in distributing relief items to those in need. I also provided free tutoring to students in underprivileged schools, helping them overcome educational challenges. I earned a bronze medal in the Duke of Edinburgh International Award, recognizing my perseverance and initiative. After my father's passing, I took on significant family responsibilities, managing our family business while continuing to support my family. Creatively, I express myself through painting landscapes and portraits, which has been a fulfilling hobby and a way to unwind. Additionally, I developed a personal portfolio and blog website, where I share insights and resources that have helped many students. My passion for education and technology also led me to provide private tutoring, using the income to support my pursuits. Furthermore, I completed an internship as an assistant chemistry teacher at my school where my work was to check and grade mock exams and take some extra classes, gaining valuable experience in teaching and mentoring students.
